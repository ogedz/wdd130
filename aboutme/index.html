About me

Monday Ogedengbe

Maccabaeus

My name is Monday Ogedengbe. I am 40 years old.

I was raised in Ibadan, Oyo state, Nigeria, where I received both my primary and secondary education.

Afterward, I proceeded to Ahmadu Bello University to obtain an A-Level Certificate.

I then went on to earn a Bachelor of Science in Economics from the University of Jos, Plateau state.

I'm a Business and Financial Analyst; a proficient Bookkeeper with adequate knowledge in Accounting software such as Quikbook.

Recently, I completed the BYU Pathway Connect program and have now enrolled at Brigham Young University in Idaho, USA, to pursue a Bachelor of Science in Software Development.

I am a strategic thinker with strong communication, listening, and technical skills.

My problem-solving abilities are excellent, and I have an advanced knowledge of Microsoft Excel, a deep understanding of the Python programming language, and exceptional data analysis skills.
